Creative Coder.

Hi there, thanks for checking out my site! I got into algorithmic art using R's ggplot (aRt as I call it) a couple of years ago. My 9-5 involves a lot of programming so it's been fun to go at it from a different angle. I mostly work in static images and the occasional gif, but recently I've started branching out into web-based projects using SVG and JavaScript. These tools are fun because they open the door to interactive animation!

There are a number of talented creative coders sharing their work on Twitter but Tyler Hobbs is one in particular I enjoy following.

Stay tuned, I'll try to regularly update the site with new projects!
